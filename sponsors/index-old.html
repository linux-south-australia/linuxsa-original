Sponsors of LinuxSA

The following is a list of companies who have contributed to getting LinuxSA to where it is at the moment.

Compaq Australia

Compaq Computer Australia (formerly Digital Equipment Corporation) kindly allow us to use their premises at 139 Frome Street for meetings.

NetCraft

NetCraft Australia specialises in customised Web Page Design and Publication Services for companies and individuals who want to publish, advertise or sell products or services on the World Wide Web. Their staff have extensive experience in web page design and production, programming and data communications. In short - all the talent and skills needed to create interesting and interactive web pages for your company. NetCraft also sell and support the Red Hat Linux operating system.

NetCraft kindly allow us to use their web space for the LinuxSA Web Pages. NetCraft also are the maintainers of the linuxsa.org.au domain.

Caldera

Caldera delivers full-featured, economical system software alternatives to individuals and enterprises. The company distinguishes itself from other technology companies by coupling open-source operating system technologies, such as Linux, with traditional industry business practices, including channel partner distribution and support; leveraged third-party application development; and internal research and development, marketing and technical support.

Caldera has kindly supplied a number of free CD's and T-Shirts to the group.

Red Hat Software

Red Hat Software is a computer software development company that sells products and provides services related to Linux, a freely available UN*X-like operating system. Linux has all the features of any full-fledged operating system, but the nature of Linux allows Red Hat to offer quality software solutions at lower cost than other software vendors.

Red Hat Software has kindly supplied a number of free CD's and T-Shirts to the group.
