Sponsors of LinuxSA

The following is a list of companies who are considered sponsors of LinuxSA.

NetCraft

NetCraft provides a range of Internet and networking solutions based on the Linux operating system and Cisco datacommunications products.

NetCraft kindly allow us to use their web space for the LinuxSA Web Pages. NetCraft also are the maintainers of the linuxsa.org.au domain.

Internode Professional Access

Internode Professional Access is South Australia's leading Internet access provider.

Internode kindly sponsor the local Red Hat Linux FTP server by providing rack space and Internet connectivity.
