16 December, 2004

  • Since RSS feeds are becoming all the more common, LinuxSA has followed the crowd and introduced its own "planet aggregator" -- Planet LinuxSA.

    Anyone South Australian who writes or enjoys Linux and/or Open-Source is welcome to have their own blog included. Just email Michael Davies <michael at msdavies dot net> with your syndication feed and he'll include you.
