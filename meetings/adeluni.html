The simplest way to find this is to find the Security Office on the plaza in the centre of the University. The Security Office is clearly marked on all the maps at the entrances to the University. Walk past BankSA and enter the Plaza Building (you should see some displays by the architecture department), walk down the stairs to level 2 and enter the computer science department doors.

The best place to park is Victoria Drive, then enter the uni opposite the footbridge and walk up the stairs to the plaza.

Maps can be found at http://www.adelaide.edu.au/campuses.html.

The Plaza Building has building code H9 and is at North Terrace Campus Map coordinate G9. It can only be entered from the western side (where the arrow is on the map).
