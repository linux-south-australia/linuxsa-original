Information Selection
      Display package information:
        • rpm -qi libc

      Display files in the package:
        • rpm -ql libc
        • Verbose: -v

      Display a package's configuration or documentation files:
        • rpm -qc libc
        • rpm -qd libc

      Custom Queries:
        • rpm -qa --queryformat '%{NAME} %{SIZE}\n' | sort -n +1
