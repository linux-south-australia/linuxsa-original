RAID5

  • Spread parity across all disks

    • Disk1: P 4 7 10
    • Disk2: 1 P 8 11
    • Disk3: 2 5 P 12
    • Disk4: 3 6 9 P

  • Preferred choice for large, mid-performance disks

  • Performance drops as disks added

  • Write caching in NVRAM can improve perfromance
